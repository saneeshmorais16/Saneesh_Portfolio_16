Favorite Interview Questions

Product & Strategy

  • What real problem does this feature solve, and for whom?
  • How would you validate it in one week with minimal engineering?
  • What’s your North Star metric and the 2–3 guardrail metrics?
  • When would you choose rules over LLMs, and why?
  • Tell me about a time a metric misled you. What did you change?

Execution & Ownership

  • Describe a 0→1 project you shipped. What made it succeed?
  • How do you de-risk unknowns before committing a full sprint?
  • What tradeoffs did you make on performance vs. delivery speed?
  • What’s your post-launch checklist (observability, alerts, rollbacks)?
  • How do you handle “good enough now” vs. “perfect later”?

AI / ML Depth

  • Walk me through your evals: datasets, metrics, and error analysis.
  • How do you prevent prompt overfitting and silent regressions?
  • RAG design: chunking, retrieval strategy, and grounding checks?
  • Hallucination controls: where do you place guardrails and why?
  • Latency & cost: where have you optimized tokens or model choice?

Data & Experimentation

  • What’s your smallest experiment to detect causal impact?
  • When A/B is impractical, how do you gain confidence?
  • Data quality: what automated checks do you set up on Day 1?
  • How do you debug “it works locally but not in prod” with data?
  • What’s a metric you stopped using? Why?

Tip: I value clarity, measurable impact, and fast feedback loops.
